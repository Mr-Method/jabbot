= Intro

Free agent writing packages, classes, prototypes, lambdas, markups, and most
difficult of all, text/plain. Free mind playing instruments, garagebands,
musics, and coffee machines; consuming only caffeine, longjump to any cafe
with no labels.

心性，愛玩樂器、garageband、音樂、咖啡機；為了吸收咖啡因，longjump 到任何小咖啡
館。
